Welcome to my homepage!

I am currently an Assistant Professor at the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Oakland University. Previously I was working with Prof. Sander Rhebergen on space-time hybridizable finite element methods (2016-2018) and at the Von Karman Institute in Belgium with Prof. Herman Daconinck on adjoint based methods for aeronautic application (2014-2016). I obtained my PhD from the Institute of Mathematics at the Eotvos Lorand University in Hungary.
